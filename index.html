Projects


Indiana University Bus Transit System Route Optimization

Data mining on modeled data from bus system, weather conditions and schedules of current semester to detect and solve delays in daily trips and dynamically deciding routes per day to maximize total time on the route. Involved finding an efficient solution to reduce the possible casues of overload of system and delays on the route.

Sentiment Analysis on Twitter

Involved collecting data (tweets) from twitter about any subject (movie, actor, etc.), pre-processing of tweets, calculating the polarity (positive or negative) of each tweet by using AVA (Adjective-Verb-Adverb) combinations and then finding out the total mean sentiment of the subject.

Data Mining using Wolberg's Breast Cancer Data

Analyzed wolberg's breast cancer dataset using kl-means clustering to classify if the tumor is benign or malignant. Achieved 98% accuracy on 10% of the dataset as test data using 10 fold cross validation.
